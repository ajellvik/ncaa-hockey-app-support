Q: What data sources does the app use?

A: We pull official data from NCAA.com for rankings and statistics, USCHO.com for weekly polls, and College Hockey News for detailed box scores. All data is updated in real-time.

üîî Notifications

Q: How do I set up notifications for my favorite team?

A: Go to Settings ‚Üí Select your favorite team ‚Üí Enable notifications. You can customize which types of alerts you want to receive and set quiet hours.

Q: I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?

A: Check that notifications are enabled in your iPhone Settings ‚Üí NCAA Hockey App ‚Üí Notifications. Also ensure you've selected a favorite team in the app settings.

üìä Rankings & Stats

Q: What are Pairwise Rankings?

A: Pairwise Rankings are the official NCAA selection criteria used to determine tournament seeding. They include RPI values and head-to-head comparisons between teams.

Q: How often are stats updated?

A: Statistics and rankings are updated in real-time as games conclude and official data becomes available from our sources.

üèí Live Games

Q: Why don't I see a game that's happening?

A: We only display NCAA Division I Men's Ice Hockey games. Exhibition games, club hockey, or other divisions may not appear. Pull down to refresh if you think a game is missing.

Q: The app is running slowly or crashing

A: Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, restart your device. For iPhone SE or older devices, the app automatically optimizes the interface for better performance.
